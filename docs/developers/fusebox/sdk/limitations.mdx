## Limitations

The FuseSDK works only on the Fuse & Fuse Sparknet networks, an EVM-based L1 blockchain. Support for other blockchains is planned for the future.

If you have any questions or feedback, please get in touch with our support team at [support@fuse.io](mailto\:support@fuse.io).
