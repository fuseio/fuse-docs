1. To set up Fuse as a new network on Metamask. Click on the network selector at the top of the app and then choose "Custom RPC" from the list:

![](/img/assMRxFOj-hfekokwcTbddggN_-MVohzwcoaqm5IBcGGhf_-MVoj0Lx1BPLqn3p7MB__image.webp)

2\. Then in the "New RPC URL" enter this address: New RPC URL: https://rpc.fuse.io

<Admonition type="info" title="Optionally you can add the full parameters:">
  **Network Details**

  Additional: [https://rpc.fuse.io](https://rpc.fuse.io)

  ChainId: 122

  Explorer: [https://explorer.fuse.io](https://explorer.fuse.io/)

  Symbol: Fuse
</Admonition>

[Click here](https://news.fuse.io/how-to-add-fuse-network-to-metamask/) for a complete step-by-step guide to adding Fuse to MetaMask. Alternatively, manually add the information below to your MetaMask wallet.&#x20;

The process is the same if another wallet requires adding Fuse Network manually, although sometimes less information may be required.
